Frequently Asked Questions

Is Tunix Music really free?

Yes! Tunix Music is completely free with no premium tiers or paywalls. All features are available to everyone.

How do I add the bot?

Click the "Add to Discord" button, select your server, authorize the permissions, and you're ready to play music!

What platforms are supported?

YouTube, Spotify, SoundCloud, Bandcamp, Twitch, and more. We're constantly adding new platforms.

Can I save my playlists?

Yes! Use the /savequeue command to save your current queue and /loadqueue to load it later.

Why is the bot not playing?

Make sure the bot has permission to connect and speak in your voice channel. Check /help for troubleshooting.

How do I report a problem?

Join our Discord support server and open a ticket in the #support channel. We'll help you quickly!
