Frequently Asked Questions

Is Tunix Music really free?

Yes! Tunix Music is completely free with no premium tiers or paywalls. All features are available to everyone forever.

How do I add the bot?

Click the "Add to Discord" button, select your server, authorize the permissions, and you're ready to play music!

What platforms are supported?

YouTube, Spotify, SoundCloud, Bandcamp, Twitch, and more. We're constantly adding new platforms based on user requests.

Can I save my playlists?

Yes! Use the /savequeue command to save your current queue and /loadqueue to load it later. You can save multiple playlists.

Why is the bot not playing?

Make sure the bot has permission to connect and speak in your voice channel. Check /help for troubleshooting tips.

How do I report a problem?

Join our Discord support server and open a ticket in the #support channel. We'll help you quickly!

Can I set up a DJ role?

Yes! Use the /dj command to assign a role that can control the music. Perfect for community servers.

What about 24/7 music?

The bot stays in your voice channel 24/7 if you want. Use /settings to enable 24/7 mode in your server.
